BOBA

What is Boba?

Boba, also known as bubble tea, is a delicious Taiwanese drink made with tea, milk, and chewy tapioca pearls. It comes in many flavors and textures, from fruity and refreshing to creamy and indulgent.

History of Boba

Bubble tea originated in Taiwan in the 1980s and quickly became a global sensation. It started as a simple mix of tea and tapioca pearls but has evolved into a creative drink with endless combinations.

Types of Boba Drinks

  • MILK TEA: Classic tea with milk and sweetened pearls.
  • FRUIT TEA: Light and refreshing, made with fruit flavors.
  • SMOOTHIES & SLUSHIES: Blended, icy, and flavorful.
  • CHEESE FOAM BOBA: Tea topped with a creamy, salty-sweet cheese foam.

Tapioca Pearls & Toppings

The chewy boba pearls are made from tapioca starch, giving them their signature texture. Other toppings include popping boba, jelly, aloe vera, and pudding.
